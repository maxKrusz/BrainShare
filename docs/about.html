Meet the Developers

Hello! We're Eason, Max, Ben, and James - a group of URI Computer Science students navigating the learning curve just like everyone else. We've come to understand that to be successful in any field, it's about staying resourceful and embracing a perpetual learning journey. In our exploration of software engineering, we set out to create something grounded - a tool where kindred spirits could come together for a shared journey of learning and skill development. BrainShare is our humble contribution, a space for mutual growth without the need for grandiosity. Join us as we build something meaningful, one small achievement at a time!
